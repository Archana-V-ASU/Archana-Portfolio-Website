Beginners' Guide to Italian Cuisine

Typography-based Cookbook






In this cookbook, beginner-friendly Italian recipes were represented using typographic visuals to give the book a playful twist. Most forms and layouts of text blocks and other elements (like the tips section), were either based on typographic forms, or those of the recipes' ingredients. The red, white, and green colour scheme represents the country's flag and the iconic caprese triad (tomatoes, cheese, and basil).


Tool(s) used: Adobe Illustrator and InDesign
