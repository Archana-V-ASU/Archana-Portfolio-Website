Blissful Blueberry Ice cream

Rococo-inspired Packaging



This project takes on contemporary ice cream packaging through a Rococo lens. The similarities in colour schemes and the overall softness of the Rococo period go well with the ice cream theme. Additionally, the Rococo era was seen as “unnecessarily pretty”, “whimsical” and “illogical”, which connects to how ice cream is a treat-- an occasional indulgence rather than a regular food item or necessity. So, I tried to make the packaging “overwhelmingly” decorative. I was specifically drawn to the Rococo era's blue porcelain patterns and pastels, and these visual elements were combined with the flavour of Ice cream I chose for this product- Blueberry, through had-drawn stylised plant motifs.

Tool(s) used: Adobe Photoshop
