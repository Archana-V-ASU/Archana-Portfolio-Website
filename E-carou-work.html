Sukhrose- Jalebi packaging

Typography-based Mithai packaging




In this project, I approached packaging in a way that highlighted typographic elements. The fictitious brand name 'Sukhrose' was inspired by the sugar molecule. It also has the words 'sukh' (happiness) and 'roz' (daily), to symbolize the value and emotion associated with sweets in India, especially considering the religious importance of prasads. The logo was inspired by a saffron flower, which has six petals, and the sucrose molecule, which has two parts, both hexagonal. The specific hues of orange and pink reflect iconic desi flavours like Kesar and Gulab respectively. Gold is occasionally used as an accent colour in the brand palette.

Tool(s) used: Adobe Illustrator and Photoshop
