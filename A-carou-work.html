Mumbai Munchies

Tri-fold Brochure about Local Food and Beverages




This tri-fold brochure is about Mumbai's gastronomic street-food experience, detailing various local dishes and where one can find them. It also includes short descriptions of each dish for beginner readers, making the brochure tourist-friendly. I used Photoshop and Illustrator to work on the graphics, and InDesign to combine the visuals and text.


Tool(s) used: Adobe Photoshop and InDesign
