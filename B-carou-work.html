Rebranding TIB (The Ice-cream Bakery)

Fictional Rebranding and Relaunch Campaign for an Ice Cream Chain




This project covered various brand collaterals for a local ice cream brand, including social media posts, video content, billboards, posters, print ads, web banners, and packaging. The swirls and rounded forms of ice cream inspired the logo redesign. An
ice cream cone represents the 'i' in the logo. I chose this form because I felt it perfectly reflects the playfulness of indulging in desserts while also adding personality to the brand's previous logo.
Scroll down to see more collaterals from this project!


Tool(s) used: Adobe Illustrator, Photoshop, and InDesign
